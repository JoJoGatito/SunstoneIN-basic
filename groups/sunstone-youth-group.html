About Us

Sunstone Youth Group (SYG) is a vibrant and supportive community for LGBTQ+ youth in Southern Colorado. We create safe spaces where young people can explore their identities, build friendships, and find mentorship within the community.

Our group focuses on empowerment, creativity, and personal growth through various activities and programs. We provide resources, support, and opportunities for youth to develop leadership skills while connecting with peers who share similar experiences.
